About TEXTBER

Textber is a new way to request UBER rides without a smartphone or internet access. All you need is a phone with text capability!

TEXTBER allows the user to get a fare estimate, and hail a UBER driver once the user provides a starting point and a end addressâ€¦.all without the use of a smartphone device.

Directions:

1. Sign up for an Uber Account at www.uber.com
2. Visit textber.co and authenticate uber account
3. Associate a phone number to your account for the first time
3. Start requesting a Uber by texting two addresses to the number sent to your phone from textber.
